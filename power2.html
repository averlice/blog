Context

In 2024, I had written an essay on the power grid, which you can check out here.

The essay outlined points about the power grids' future demise, its flames, and its vulnerabilities.

Has the situation gotten any better?

Negative!

In fact, it has gotten worse this year.

Let me just point out to you how it has gotten worse.

Using power meters as surveillance tools

Let’s start off in California.

This article comes from the electronic frontier foundation

Title: “When Your Power Meter Becomes a Tool of Mass Surveillance“

Police in San Diego, California were using smart electric meters as surveillance tools. They suspected anyone using more than the normal rate of electricity, such as to power Christmas lights, or even medical equipment, of committing a crime.

From electronic frontier foundation article:

“Sacramento’s power company and law enforcement agencies have been running an illegal mass surveillance scheme for years, using our power meters as home-mounted spies. The Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF) is seeking to end Sacramento’s dragnet surveillance of energy customers and have asked for a court order to stop this practice for good.”

Now do I think EFF will be able to get a court order?

Knowing how much of a dumpster fire it is becoming, no, I don't. But who knows…

Ferc electric reliability assessment

ferc electric reliability assessment

Federal energy regulatory commission

Cyberattacks may have nothing to do with weather, but they sure are ,shocking, and can, flood homes with power outages. And boy, have cyberattacks, stormed, up.

From the assessment:

“The threat of physical and cyberattacks on the grid continue to pose a concern in summer 2025, especially at times when the electric grid could be under stress due to higher electricity demand and extreme temperatures. Attacks that compromise substations or transmission infrastructure could exacerbate supply constraints, leading to localized outages or even cascading failures.”

Oy. let the cyberattacks rain, on us I guess.

Ok, ok, I'll stop with the weather jokes.

Nevertheless, all jokes aside, this is a threat, and we have seen many utility companies get hacked. And when they get hacked, they have access to the one switch your life depends on : the power switch.

In fact, on April 25th, 2025, Nova Scotia Power announced they had a cyberattack.

“April 28, 2025 – Emera Inc. and Nova Scotia Power today announced on April 25, 2025 they discovered and are actively responding to a cybersecurity incident involving unauthorized access into certain parts of its Canadian network and servers supporting portions of its business applications.”

Parochial and space blackout

Report by entsoe

“On 28 April 2025, at 12:33 CEST, the power systems of Spain and Portugal experienced a blackout. A small area in France, close to the border with Spain, was also affected for a limited duration.”

Even today, the causes are still unknown to this day.

What this tells me

This tells me that this is not going to get any better anytime soon. In fact, something tells me it’s going to get worse.

The only way to truly mitigate this is off grid living. Solar panels, wind turbines, batteries, everything from there.
